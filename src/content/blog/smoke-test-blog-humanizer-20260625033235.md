## Why reusable humanization matters

Drafting tools are helpful, but their initial output often feels like it was pieced together from a collection of safe, generic sentence fragments. This creates a bottleneck in the review process, as someone still needs to step in and rewrite the draft to make it sound credible, direct, and human.

A reusable humanizer step solves this at the workflow level, so you aren't relying on team members to remember a cleanup prompt every time. When that same rewrite pass is integrated into your blog, email, and social workflows, your output stays consistent and the overall editorial workload drops significantly.

This disposable post exists only to confirm that the blog PR workflow now triggers that rewrite step before committing markdown into GitHub. Once you’ve validated this, the branch and PR can be removed.
